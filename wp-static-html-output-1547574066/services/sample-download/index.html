Using Downloads (See the on the Menu)

  1. Go to Downloads (left side Menu bar)
  2. Click ‘All Download’ -> ‘Add New’
  3. Enter the title of file/document
  4. Choose file that want to upload at ‘Upload File’
  5. Click ‘Publish’ at the right side bar
  6. Back to the ‘All Download’
  7. Copy the shortcode
  8. Open Page that you want to use. For example this page (Download:Sample)
  9. Click ‘Add Block’ -> ‘ Widgets’ – [/] and paste the shortcode

One topic has many files (Using Downloads assign Category)

  1. Go to Downloads (left side Menu bar)
  2. Click ‘All Download’ -> ‘Download Categories’
  3. Fill up the Name, Slugs (for shortcode) and description(Optional)
  4. For slugs, just copy the name. Avoid space by replace ‘_’ or ‘-‘
  5. Click ‘Add New Category’
  6. Copy the shortcode
  7. Open Page that you want to use. For example this page (Download:Sample)
  8. Click ‘Add Block’ -> ‘ Widgets’ – [/] and paste the shortcode
